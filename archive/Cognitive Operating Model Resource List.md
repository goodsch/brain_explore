# Cognitive Operating Model Resource List
A curated list of books, assessments, and tools to understand how your brain learns, thinks, and organizes.

---

## Books — Learning, Cognition, and Thinking Patterns
- How We Learn — Benedict Carey
- The Art of Learning — Josh Waitzkin
- A Mind for Numbers — Barbara Oakley
- Thinking, Fast and Slow — Daniel Kahneman
- Thinking Styles — Robert Sternberg
- The Developing Mind — Daniel Siegel
- Cognitive Styles and Learning Strategies — Riding & Rayner
- Smart but Scattered (Adults) — Dawson & Guare
- Delivered from Distraction — Hallowell & Ratey
- The Distracted Mind — Gazzaley & Rosen
- Metacognition (Dunlosky & Metcalfe — Handbook chapter)
- Peak — Anders Ericsson
- Atomic Habits — James Clear

---

## Assessments — Cognitive Style, EF, Metacognition

### Cognitive Preference & Thinking Style
- HBDI (Herrmann Brain Dominance Instrument)
- Kolb Learning Style Inventory
- VIA Character Strengths

### Executive Function & Working Memory
- BRIEF-A
- WAIS-IV: Working Memory Index
- WAIS-IV: Processing Speed Index
- Conners Adult ADHD Rating Scale (CAARS)

### Metacognition & Study Strategies
- Metacognitive Awareness Inventory (MAI)
- Learning and Study Strategies Inventory (LASSI)
- Motivated Strategies for Learning Questionnaire (MSLQ)

### Cognitive Tracking
