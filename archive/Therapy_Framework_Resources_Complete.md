# Complete Resource Library for Therapy Framework Development

A comprehensive collection of books, tools, assessments, and resources for:
- Understanding how you think, learn, and process information
- Building an integrative therapeutic model
- Designing AI-facilitated questioning and reflection systems
- Grounding your framework in research and evidence

---

## Part I: Cognitive Architecture & How You Think

Books and frameworks for understanding memory, reasoning, biases, emotional integration, and information processing.

### Core Cognitive Books

| Book | Author | Focus Area | Why It's Useful |
|------|--------|------------|-----------------|
| The Feeling of What Happens | Antonio Damasio | Emotion, identity, memory, decision-making | Understanding the link between emotion and cognition; helps design effective questioning |
| How We Learn | Benedict Carey | Retention, forgetting, retrieval, mental models | Informs session structure, pacing, recall prompts, and sequencing |
| Thinking, Fast and Slow | Daniel Kahneman | Biases, heuristics, dual systems | Predicting reasoning shortcuts; building agent prompts that challenge gently |
| Surfing Uncertainty | Andy Clark | Predictive processing | Designing questions around assumptions, priors, and meaning-making |
| The Mind is Flat | Nick Chater | Constructed cognition | Questions that generate new connections rather than retrieve fixed ones |
| The Extended Mind | Annie Murphy Paul | Environment, tools, external cognition | Shaping visual UI, mind mapping, and SiYuan workflows |
| Metaphors We Live By | Lakoff & Johnson | Conceptual metaphor structure | Understanding how questions manipulate metaphoric structure |
| A Mind for Numbers | Barbara Oakley | Mode-switching, understanding blocks | Designing how the agent handles stuck moments |

### Neuroscience & Emotion

| Book | Author | Focus Area |
|------|--------|------------|
| Self Comes to Mind | Antonio Damasio | Consciousness, self, emotion |
| The Evolved Self | Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi | Flow, identity development |
| The Influence of Emotions on Cognition | Various | Research reviews on emotion-cognition interaction |

## Part XIV: Reading Roadmap Suggestion

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
- Behave (Sapolsky)
- How Emotions Are Made (Barrett)
- Thinking, Fast and Slow (Kahneman)

### Phase 2: Self-Understanding (Weeks 5-8)
- The Extended Mind (Paul)
- A More Beautiful Question (Berger)
- Complete cognitive assessments (Big Five, VIA, Kolb, CRT)

### Phase 3: Therapeutic Model (Weeks 9-12)
- Motivational Interviewing (Miller & Rollnick)
- Internal Family Systems (Schwartz)
- The Body Keeps the Score (van der Kolk)

### Phase 4: Integration (Weeks 13-16)
- Oxford Handbook of Psychotherapy Integration
- The Great Psychotherapy Debate (Wampold)
- Maps of Meaning (Peterson) — for structure

### Phase 5: Meaning & Identity (Ongoing)
- Man's Search for Meaning (Frankl)
- The Denial of Death (Becker)
- Narrative resources as needed
