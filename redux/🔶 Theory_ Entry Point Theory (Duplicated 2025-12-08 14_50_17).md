## 1. Summary

Entry Point Theory states that meaningful thinking cannot begin from a blank state.  
It requires a **contextual hook** ‚Äî an "entry point" ‚Äî for cognition to activate.

This is a neurological and phenomenological characteristic of ADHD and nonlinear thinkers.

Thinking begins only when there is *something to grab onto*.
