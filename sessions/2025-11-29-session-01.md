## Key Insights

1. **The Narrow Window of Awareness** — We're aware enough to create meaning from limited data, but not aware enough to see our blind spots. This is the root of both suffering AND the capacity for meaning.

2. **Meaning-Making is a Solution** — Not good or bad, just adaptive. 5 senses + pattern-matching brain = "knowing." Better than walking into walls.

3. **The Paradox of Efficiency** — A more advanced intelligence might see us as inefficient (fighting wars over skin color). But that "inefficiency" IS the capacity for beauty, love, passion.

4. **Incorrectness IS Correctness** — The ignorant creation of "false" meaning IS meaning. Our limitation is our gift.

5. **Therapy Isn't About "More Correct"** — It's about recognizing where beliefs cause UNNECESSARY pain, allowing unique contribution, validating all paths.

6. **People Matter Simply Because** — No justification needed. The brain creates a singular person who matters by existing.
